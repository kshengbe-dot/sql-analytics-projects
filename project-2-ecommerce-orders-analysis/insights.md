# E-commerce Orders Analysis Insights

## Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and revenue generation in an e-commerce database.

## Key Insights

### 1. High-Value Customers
Eva Green emerged as the top customer with a total spend of $1500.

This indicates that a small number of customers can contribute significantly to total revenue.

### 2. Revenue Drivers
High-priced items such as laptops contributed heavily to total revenue, showing that premium products are key revenue drivers.

### 3. Customer Concentration Risk
Revenue is somewhat concentrated among a few customers, which could pose a risk if those customers churn.

### 4. Business Opportunity
The company can:
- Target similar high-value customers
- Offer loyalty incentives
- Focus marketing on premium products

### 5. Order Insights
Completed orders are the main source of revenue, while cancelled orders represent lost sales opportunities.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Multi-table joins
- Revenue calculations
- Customer lifetime value analysis
- Business insight generation
